Join the Apache HoraeDB community

Apache HoraeDB is an open source project that anyone in the community can use, improve, and enjoy. We'd love you to join us! Here's a few ways to find out what's happening and get involved.

Learn and Connect

Using or want to use Apache HoraeDB? Find out more here:

  • GitHub Discussions: Discussion and help from your fellow users
  • Weixin Official Accounts: Follow us on Weixin Official Accounts（微信公众平台） to get the latest news!

Develop and Contribute

If you want to get more involved by contributing to Apache HoraeDB, join us here:

  • GitHub: Development takes place here!
  • DingTalk: Chat with other project developers on DingTalk
  • Developer mailing list: Discuss development issues around the project

You can find out how to contribute to Apache HoraeDB in our Contribution Guidelines.

Apache HoraeDB is an effort undergoing incubation at The Apache Software Foundation (ASF), sponsored by the Apache Incubator. Incubation is required of all newly accepted projects until a further review indicates that the infrastructure, communications, and decision making process have stabilized in a manner consistent with other successful ASF projects. While incubation status is not necessarily a reflection of the completeness or stability of the code, it does indicate that the project has yet to be fully endorsed by the ASF.
